# Winner Circle Lottery Pool, Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 29, 2025

## Data Collection
Winner Circle Lottery Pool does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  Because you are not my product, this app is :)
  
## Local Storage
All information including your saved picks and pool data stays on your device only.

## Analytics & Tracking
We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  Again, you are not my product, this app is :)

## Contacts Permission
  The app offers “Add from Contacts”, iOS may ask permission to access your contacts.
      If granted, Winner Circle Lottery Pool uses contact names only to help you add pool members. Contacts information is not collected outside of your device or used outside the intended purpose of adding members to the list efficiently.
